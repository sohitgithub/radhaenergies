Leadership

Building Clean Energy for Every Community

Anurag Mishra

Founder, Radha Energies

Radha Energies was founded by Anurag Mishra with a clear purpose — to bring dependable solar power to homes and businesses across Madhya Pradesh and beyond. From our base in Jaso, Nagod, we work closely with every client to design systems that reduce electricity costs and support a sustainable future.

We believe solar is not just technology; it is long-term partnership. That is why we focus on quality installations, honest guidance, and support you can reach by phone whenever you need us.
